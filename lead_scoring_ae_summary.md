## How Scores Are Built

**Your lead score = FIT + ENGAGEMENT**

- **Fit Score** = Company characteristics (size, type, revenue, staff)
- **Engagement Score** = Actions taken (emails, meetings, forms, events)

**Example Hot Lead (1,500+ points):**
- Church with 800 attendance (+10)
- $8M annual revenue (+10)
- 75 employees (+10)
- Broadcasting on TV & radio (+20)
- 50K monthly listeners (+10)
- Using CRM tools (+5)
- Attended marketing event (+50)
- Completed meeting (+50)
- Replied to sales email (+15)
- Timeline: 1-3 months (+50)
- Budget: $2M+ (+20)
- Decision maker engaged (+20)
- **Total: 270+ points from shown criteria alone**

**Example Disqualified Lead (-75 points):**
- Competitor (-5)
- Under $1M revenue (-5)
- Under 10 employees (-5)
- No distribution channels (-10)
- Just planning content strategy (-10)
- Not looking to grow (-50)
- **Total: -85 points**

## Common Questions

**Q: Why is my lead scored high but not responding?**
**A:** High fit score â‰  high engagement. They match our ICP but haven't shown buying intent yet. Use the fit signals to personalize outreach about their specific situation.

**Q: Lead has low score but is asking for demo. What do I do?**
**A:** Take the meeting! Score is predictive, not absolute. Quick qualification to confirm they're not disqualified, then proceed. Update qualification fields to boost score.

**Q: Score seems wrong. Can I override it?**
**A:** Contact Growth Ops to review. Score is based on data in HubSpot, so might indicate missing information rather than wrong score.

**Q: How often do scores update?**
**A:** Real-time as data changes. When someone opens an email, submits a form, or you update their record, score adjusts immediately.

**Q: What score should I expect to close?**
**A:** 500+ at engagement, 1,000+ at proposal stage. If proposing to sub-500 lead, qualification might be weak.
